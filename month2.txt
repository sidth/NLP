March is the third month of the year. Starts with M.
The name of March comes from Martius, the first month of the earliest Roman calendar.
to move along steadily usually with a rhythmic stride and in step with others
In Slovene, the traditional name is sušec, meaning the month when the earth becomes dry enough so that it is possible to cultivate it. The name was first written in 1466 in the Škofja Loka manuscript.
to move in a direct purposeful manner. March is the third month of the year.
In Finnish, the month is called maaliskuu, which is believed to originate from maallinen kuu, during March, earth finally becomes visible under the snow. March is the third month of the year.
