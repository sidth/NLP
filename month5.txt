June is the sixth month of the year.
The Latin name for June is Junius. Ovid offers multiple etymologies for the name in the Fasti, a poem about the Roman calendar. The first is that the name is named after the Roman goddess Juno. June is the sixth month of the year. 
June's birthstones are pearl, alexandrite and moonstone. June is the sixth month in the Julian and Gregorian calendars.
The zodiac signs for the month of June are Gemini (until June 20) and Cancer (from June 21 onwards).
